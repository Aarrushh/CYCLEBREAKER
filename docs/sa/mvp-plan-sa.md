# South Africa MVP Plan

Objective
- Deliver real value within 6–8 weeks without heavy integrations, while building credibility and coverage.

Week 1–2: Spike
- Build PWA skeleton with offline cache and English.
- WhatsApp bot MVP (FAQ, directions, grant checklist).
- Adapters: SASSA payment calendars; NSFAS deadlines; 2 SETA pages; 3 municipal clinic directories.
- Community submission form + moderation v0.

Week 3–4: MVP core
- Cycle Breaker Score + urgency alerts per module.
- Grants/Training/Jobs feeds; printable forms; SMS reminders.
- Parallel search templates; DeepSeek extraction prompts and rule DSL applied to SA sources.

Week 5–6: Trust and scale
- Multilingual (Zulu or Xhosa); “freshness verified” labels; contributor reputation.
- Transport-aware listings (walking radius) and offline map tiles.
- Ambassadors pilot (5–10) + Airtime incentives.

Week 7–8: Partnerships and zero-rating
- Approach 1–2 MNOs for zero-rating pilot; NGO partnerships for content supply.
- Expand source adapters; add price ranges with confidence.

Exit criteria
- 500–1,500 opportunities/services in SA knowledge base.
- P50 user can find 1+ relevant opportunity within first session.
- <30s time-to-first-result on low-end device; app usable fully offline after warm cache.
