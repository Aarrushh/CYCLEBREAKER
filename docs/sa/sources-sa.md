# South Africa Sources

Official and high-signal sources to seed the SA knowledge base and search adapters.

Government and agencies
- SASSA (sassa.gov.za) — grants, payment calendars, requirements
- SA Government (gov.za) — services, benefits, how-to pages
- NSFAS (nsfas.org.za) — student funding, deadlines, requirements
- SETAs (sector education & training authorities) — learnerships, bursaries, training
  - W&RSETA, MICTSETA, CETA, HWSETA, etc.
- Department of Employment and Labour (labour.gov.za) — programs, UIF, training
- Local municipalities — clinics, social services, soup kitchens, shelters, community centers

NGOs and programs
- Harambee / SA Youth Mobi — youth jobs and opportunities
- Gift of the Givers — relief, health outreach
- Partners for Possibility, local NPOs — community services

Education and training
- TVET colleges — course calendars and admissions info
- Universities and university short courses (with bursaries)

Jobs and gig economy
- SweepSouth — domestic work marketplace
- Job Jack — entry-level job listings
- Giraffe, CareerJunction (filtered for entry-level)
- Local Facebook/WhatsApp community groups (curated and moderated)

Health
- Provincial health departments — clinic locations/hours, advisories
- Pharmacy chains — Dis-Chem, Clicks (in-store clinic hours and promotions)

Notes
- Prefer official APIs or RSS where available; otherwise scrape under robots/ToS allowances.
- Track freshness (last-checked), source quality, and change deltas.
